A Tribute to Mohbad

Aloba Promise Ilerioluwa Oladimeji, popularly known as Mohbad (Imole), is a songwriter, performer, rapper and singer. Rising to fame in 2019, following series of freestyle and later Hit songs like KPK, Feel Good, Back Side, Ronaldo, and lots

Mohbad is known for his unique sound and his captivating live performances. He is one of the most popular artists in Nigeria right now, and his music has been enjoyed by fans all over the world.

Mohbad is a talented artist who is passionate about his music. He is also a role model for many young people, and he uses his platform to speak out about important social issues.

In recent months, Mohbad has been the victim of harassment. He has been subjected to physical and psychological torture. Despite the challenges he has faced, Mohbad has remained strong and determined. He continues to make music and to speak out against injustice.

I am writing this tribute to Mohbad to show my support for him and to raise awareness of the injustice he has faced. I urge everyone to stand with Mohbad and to demand justice for him. We must not allow artists like Mohbad to be silenced.
